mic Voice Orders

Tap the mic icon and speak naturally. Try these commands:

  • Custom Order: "Add one Iced Latte with Oat Milk and Less Sweet."
  • Multi-Order: "I want two Hot Americanos, one Croissant, and a Turon."
  • Adjust Qty: "Increase my Americano by one" or "Decrease the Croissant."
  • Inquiries: "What are the ingredients of the Tapsilog?" or "Do you sell doughnuts?"
